Admission Requirements

We welcome students who demonstrate academic potential, leadership qualities, and commitment to excellence. Our admission process is transparent and student-centered.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Age-appropriate for the applying class
  • Previous academic records
  • Entrance examination (if applicable)
  • Personal interview and aptitude assessment
  • School transfer certificate from previous institution
  • Character certificate and health records

Admission Process

1

Application Form — Submit complete application with required documents

2

Entrance Test — Evaluation of academic aptitude and learning capability

3

Personal Interview — Meet with admission counselors to assess fit

4

Merit List — Selection based on comprehensive evaluation

5

Admission Confirmation — Fee deposit and official enrollment

Admission Deadline: May 30, 2026

Classes Offered: Class 6 to Class 12
